About CyberSmrt

A Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned 501(c)(3) nonprofit bridging the cybersecurity equity gap through accessible education and affordable services for underserved communities.

Who We Are

CyberSmrt was founded on the belief that cybersecurity shouldn't be a privilege reserved for well-funded organizations and affluent communities. With 15+ years of Navy cybersecurity experience, we saw firsthand how the digital divide leaves vulnerable populations exposed to increasingly sophisticated cyber threats.

We're changing that by delivering high-quality cybersecurity education to K-12 students in underserved schools, providing affordable managed security services to nonprofits and small businesses, and creating workforce development pathways for adults from disadvantaged backgrounds.
