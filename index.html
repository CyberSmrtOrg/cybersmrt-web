Our Mission

CyberSmrt bridges the cybersecurity equity gap by delivering accessible, high-quality education and affordable cybersecurity services to nonprofits, schools, and small businesses. We envision a future where every community—regardless of size or resources—has the knowledge and protection to thrive in an increasingly digital world.

Who We Serve

Cybersecurity shouldn't be a privilege—it's a necessity. We focus on communities that have been left behind in the digital revolution.

🏫

Under-Resourced Schools

Inner-city and rural schools that lack cybersecurity education programs and can't afford enterprise security solutions.

🤝

Community Nonprofits

Mission-driven organizations protecting sensitive donor and beneficiary data without the budget for dedicated security staff.

🏪

Small Businesses

Local businesses that keep communities alive but face 350% more cyberattacks than larger firms—with no security budget.

👶

Youth & Families

Young people from underserved communities who deserve pathways to high-paying cybersecurity careers, not just awareness of threats.

👵

Seniors & Elderly

Older adults who are disproportionately targeted by scams and need age-appropriate digital safety education.

🌾

Rural Communities

Remote areas where broadband is just arriving but cybersecurity education and support are still years behind.

The Equity Gap is Real

47% of companies with fewer than 50 employees have NO cybersecurity budget
27% of nonprofits report experiencing a cyber breach due to limited awareness
41% of small businesses experienced a cyberattack in the last 12 months

We meet communities where they are. Our volunteer-driven, mission-first approach enables flexible pricing, sliding-scale services, and free resources for those who need them most.

Our Programs

🎓

K-12 Cybersecurity Curriculum

Modular, competency-based cybersecurity education for grades 5-12, co-developed with NEAAAT charter school. Aligned with industry standards and designed for scalable licensing.

✓ Hands-on, project-based learning
✓ Portfolio-driven assessment
✓ Family engagement components
🛡️

MSSP-lite Services

Affordable managed security services for nonprofits and small businesses, including risk assessments, penetration testing, and cybersecurity awareness training.

✓ Enterprise-grade tools (Tenable)
✓ Accessible pricing models
✓ Future integration with sIQurity AI
💼

Workforce Development

Adult training programs aligned with industry certifications (CompTIA, ISC2, GIAC) across three tracks: Red Team, Blue Team, and GRC.

✓ Hands-on labs and simulations
✓ Mentorship and internship placements
✓ Job readiness support
🌐

Community Outreach

Targeted programs for underserved populations: digital literacy for seniors, youth workshops in inner-city schools, rural community training, and multilingual resources to ensure no one is left behind in the digital age.

✓ Age-inclusive & culturally responsive
✓ Free resources in multiple languages
✓ Inner-city & rural community focus
👨‍🏫

Train-the-Trainer

Empowering educators with structured onboarding, teaching simulations, and micro-credentialing to deliver high-quality cybersecurity content independently.

✓ Confidence-building modules
✓ Peer feedback cycles
✓ Ongoing professional development
🚀

Strategic Partnerships

Building relationships with ISC2, CompTIA, SANS, ISACA, Cloud Range, and technology vendors to ensure alignment, recognition, and co-branded offerings.

✓ Certification pathways
✓ Tool sponsorships
✓ Industry collaboration
